He lives to bless me with His love; Glory hallelujah!He lives to plead my cause above; Glory hallelujah! He lives my hungry soul to feed; Glory hallelujah!He lives to help in time of need; Glory hallelujah!

He lives, my kind and gracious friend; Glory hallelujah!He lives and loves me to the end; Glory hallelujah! He lives, and while He lives I'll sing; Glory hallelujah! He lives, my Prophet, Priest and King; Glory hallelujah!
